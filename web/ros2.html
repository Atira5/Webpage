ROS2 & Robotics Development Program

Accelerate your robotics career with Evolve Robotics’ industry-focused ROS2 & Robotics Development Program. Designed for aspiring robotics engineers and automation enthusiasts, this program equips learners with practical expertise in robot operating systems, robotic communication frameworks, simulation, sensor integration, autonomous navigation, and intelligent robotic application development, preparing them for real-world robotics and automation projects.

• 📚 3 Hrs/Day   •   📁 Projects   •   💼 Placement Support   •   🎓 Certification
🚀 We offer 3-hour instructor-led classes, 3 days a week, along with dedicated project support and active mentorship on the remaining days — ensuring continuous learning and real-world experience.
