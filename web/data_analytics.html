Professional Program in Data Analytics & Power BI

Transform your career with Evolve Robotics’ industry-focused Data Analytics program.

Designed for beginners and aspiring professionals, this course equips you with the essential skills to analyze data, uncover meaningful insights, and support real-world business decisions.

Through hands-on projects, real-world datasets, and expert mentorship, you will gain practical experience with industry-standard tools such as Python, SQL, Excel, and Power BI — making you job-ready from day one.

Not every career in technology requires deep coding expertise. At Evolve Robotics, we help you discover the right path by combining analytical thinking with modern tools, empowering you to build a strong and future-ready career in the IT industry.
